Welcome to the shekar Dairy farm

Shekar's Dairy Milk Welcome to Shekar's Dairy Milk, where we believe in delivering the freshest and purest milk straight from our farm to your home.


Owned and operated by Shekar, our dairy farm prides itself on producing high-quality milk through sustainable and ethical farming practices. Our Story Shekar's passion for dairy farming began many years ago. Driven by a commitment to quality and a love for animals, he established Shekar's Dairy Milk with the goal of providing natural and wholesome milk to families. Today, our farm is a symbol of excellence and trust in the community. Our Products At Shekar's Dairy Milk, we offer a variety of dairy products to meet your family's needs: Fresh Milk: Our milk is sourced from healthy, well-cared-for cows and processed under strict hygienic conditions to ensure its freshness and purity. Yogurt: Made from our rich and creamy milk, our yogurt is a perfect blend of taste and nutrition. Butter: Our butter is churned to perfection, adding a delicious flavor to your meals. Ghee: Made from pure milk, our ghee is rich in taste and packed with health benefits.
